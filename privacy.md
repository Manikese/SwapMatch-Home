  <h1>Privacy Policy for SwapMatch</h1>

  <p><strong>Effective Date:</strong> March 16, 2026</p>

  <p>
    SwapMatch is designed to help people discover nearby goods and services while minimizing the amount of personal information collected. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app uses and how it is handled.
  </p>

  <hr />

  <h2>1. Information We Collect</h2>

  <p>SwapMatch collects only the information needed to operate the service:</p>

  <ul>
    <li>An <strong>anonymous device ID</strong> generated on your device</li>
    <li><strong>Item listings</strong> you create</li>
    <li>Your <strong>device location</strong> (used only for proximity matching with nearby users)</li>
    <li>Optionally a <strong>phone number</strong>, if you choose to share it with a match</li>
  </ul>

  <p>SwapMatch does not require account registration or personal profiles.</p>

  <hr />

  <h2>2. How We Use Information</h2>

  <p>The information collected is used solely to operate the core functionality of the app.</p>

  <ul>
    <li>The <strong>device ID</strong> helps maintain anonymous identity within the system.</li>
    <li><strong>Location data</strong> is used only to find nearby matches within a selected radius.</li>
    <li>A <strong>phone number</strong>, if provided, is shared only with users you explicitly choose to contact.</li>
  </ul>

  <p>SwapMatch does not use this information for advertising or behavioral profiling.</p>

  <hr />

  <h2>3. Data Sharing</h2>

  <p>SwapMatch is designed to minimize data sharing.</p>

  <ul>
    <li>SwapMatch <strong>does not sell user data</strong>.</li>
    <li>SwapMatch <strong>does not use advertising trackers</strong>.</li>
    <li>SwapMatch <strong>does not use third-party advertising networks</strong>.</li>
    <li>Phone numbers are shared <strong>only when a user explicitly chooses to share contact information with a match</strong>.</li>
  </ul>

  <hr />

  <h2>4. Data Retention</h2>

  <p>Item listings and location data are retained while you use the service.</p>

  <p>Some settings and preferences may be stored locally on your device using local storage.</p>

  <p>
    Users may delete their data by <strong>uninstalling the application</strong>, which removes the local device identifier and stored preferences.
  </p>

  <hr />

  <h2>5. Contact</h2>

  <p>If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact:</p>

  <p><strong>SwapMatch@proton.me</strong></p>
