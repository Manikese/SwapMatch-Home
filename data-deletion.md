  <h1>Data Deletion Request</h1>

  <p><strong>Effective Date:</strong> March 19, 2026</p>

  <hr />

  <h2>Request Deletion of Your Data</h2>

  <p>
    SwapMatch does not use traditional user accounts. Instead, the app operates using
    an anonymous device-based system with optional contact sharing.
  </p>

  <p>
    If you would like to request deletion of your data, please contact us at:
    <strong>SwapMatch@proton.me</strong>
  </p>

  <p>
    To help us locate your data, please include the <strong>phone number</strong> you used
    within the app (if you have shared one).
  </p>

  <hr />

  <h2>What Data Can Be Deleted</h2>

  <p>
    Upon request, we will delete data associated with the provided phone number,
    including:
  </p>

  <ul>
    <li>Shared contact information</li>
    <li>Associated item listings (where identifiable)</li>
    <li>Match records connected to that contact information</li>
  </ul>

  <hr />

  <h2>What Happens If You Uninstall the App</h2>

  <p>
    Uninstalling SwapMatch removes locally stored app data from your device,
    including preferences and locally cached information.
  </p>

  <p>
    However, uninstalling the app alone does not guarantee removal of server-side data.
    To request deletion of server-side data, please contact
    <strong>SwapMatch@proton.me</strong>.
  </p>

  <hr />

  <h2>Limitations</h2>

  <p>
    Because SwapMatch does not require user accounts and primarily uses anonymous device identifiers,
    we may not be able to locate or delete data that cannot be reasonably linked to the
    information provided in your request.
  </p>

  <hr />

  <h2>Processing Time</h2>

  <p>
    We will process deletion requests within a reasonable timeframe after receiving
    sufficient information to identify the relevant data.
  </p>

  <hr />

  <h2>Contact</h2>

  <p>
    For data deletion or privacy-related questions, contact:
    <strong>SwapMatch@proton.me</strong>
  </p>
